Carrot Slices

Description

Chinese carrot slices, always made by mom

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Ground Beef
  • Vegetable Oil
    •

    Preperation

    1. Peel carrot skin
    2. Dice carrots into strands

    Instructions

    1. Pour plenty of oil, let it simmer until it boils
    2. Put ground meat in, stir until done
    3. Dump carrots inside, stir until done
    4. Add salt
