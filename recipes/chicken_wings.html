Chicken Wings

Description

Air Fryer chicken wings, homemade recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Spices
    •

    Preperation

    1. Wash chicken thoroughly
    2. Cut one/two slices in middle of chicken (to get the spices in)
    3. Pour spices in, distribute to chicken evenly

    Frying

    1. 8 minutes (180 C)
    2. Turn chicken around, another 8 minutes (180 C)
